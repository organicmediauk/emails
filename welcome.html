About Elizabeth Xi Bauer

 

Founded in 2015, Elizabeth Xi Bauer began as an online platform with pop-up exhibitions. In 2021, it opened a permanent gallery in Deptford, South-East London, and has since collaborated internationally with artists and institutions in cities like São Paulo, Amsterdam, and Lisbon.

 

In 2025, the gallery expanded to a second space in Clerkenwell’s Exmouth Market to mark its 10th anniversary, reinforcing its commitment to contemporary art and global talent.

 

Elizabeth Xi Bauer has helped launch artists like Theodore Ereira-Guyer and Abraham Kritzman, and represents international names including Antonio Pichillá. Its artists have exhibited globally and are held in major collections including the Tate, V&A, and British Museum.

How We Help Our Clients

 

Elizabeth Xi Bauer provides collectors and investors with direct access to museum-quality art. This access to validated artists has contributed to our clients seeing significant increases in the value of their artworks.

 

Through the Artistic Programme, Elizabeth Xi Bauer builds awareness and demand from the Art world experts, who run the most respected institutions. Our research clearly indicates this is the most vital factor for artists and their markets to stand the test of time.
